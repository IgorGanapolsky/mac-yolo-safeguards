Hermes Mobile is the mobile control plane for Cursor, Claude Code, OpenClaw, and Hermes gateway operators. Chat with your agent, review blocked commands, and tap Approve or Deny before anything dangerous runs — from your couch, on cellular, or over Tailscale.

NOT A PHONE AI APP
Unlike apps that run models on your phone, Hermes Mobile remote-controls the agents already running on your computer. Your API keys stay on your hardware. Pair with a QR scan — no cloud account required.
