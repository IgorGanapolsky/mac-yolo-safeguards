Hermes Mobile: AI Agent is the phone control plane for a Hermes AI agent on your own Mac, Linux, or Windows computer — not on-device phone AI. Chat with your agent from your phone, pair over Home Wi‑Fi, Tailscale, USB, or QR, see honest connection state, and approve or deny risky commands with Leash before they execute.

Search Hermes Mobile, AI agent, remote Mac agent, Tailscale AI control, or coding agent phone control — this app is remote desktop-agent supervision, not a phone chatbot.

Hermes Mobile is built for developers whose workflows also involve Cursor, Claude Code, ChatGPT or OpenAI Codex, Gemini CLI, Replit Agent, GitHub Copilot, Windsurf, OpenClaw, and other coding agents.

Hermes Mobile does not sign in to or replace those products. It supervises the Hermes agent and tools running on your own computer.

HOW THIS DIFFERS FROM OTHER “HERMES” APPS

• Termux (Android CLI): Runs Hermes on your phone. Hermes Mobile does not — your agent stays on your computer for better battery, persistence, and full tools.

• Hermes Agent (Hen Works): AI on your Android phone. Hermes Mobile is the opposite — remote control for desktop agents on Mac, Linux, or Windows.

• Hermes-Relay (cloud relay): Routes through a cloud “agent in pocket.” Hermes Mobile pairs directly via QR, Home Wi‑Fi, or Tailscale — no cloud credits.

• Hermex / HermesPilot (native clients): Other Method A apps (WebUI URL + password or Hermes Link relay). Hermes Mobile pairs with QR, Leash approvals, and honest connection state.

FAQ

• Do I need Termux? No. Hermes runs on your Mac, Linux, or Windows computer; this app is the remote control.

• Is this Hermes Agent (the popular Play app)? No — that runs AI on Android. We control desktop agents.

• Hermex or HermesPilot? Those are other native clients. We pair directly plus ThumbGate Leash.

• Do I need a cloud account? No Hermes Mobile account required. Optional $4.99 once Leash unlock for tool approvals.
