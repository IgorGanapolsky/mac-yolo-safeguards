Looking for “Hermes Agent” that runs AI on your phone? This is different.

Hermes Mobile is the phone remote for a Hermes agent on YOUR Mac, Linux, or Windows — Wi‑Fi/LAN at home, Tailscale when you leave. Chat with your gateway, see honest connection and run state (no fake green), switch saved machines, and use Leash Pro to approve or deny risky tool calls before they execute. Your API keys stay on your computer.

Independent control plane for a Hermes gateway you operate. Built for developers who also use Cursor, Claude Code, ChatGPT/Codex, Gemini CLI, Replit Agent, GitHub Copilot, Windsurf, OpenClaw, and other coding agents. Hermes Mobile does not sign in to or replace those products — it supervises the Hermes agent on your own gateway from your phone, not a generic cloud chatbot on Android.

HOW THIS DIFFERS FROM OTHER “HERMES” APPS

• Termux: runs Hermes on your phone. Hermes Mobile keeps the gateway on your computer.
• Hermes Agent (Hen Works — Play #1 for “hermes agent”): AI ON Android. Hermes Mobile is the opposite — remote control for YOUR Mac/Linux/Windows Hermes gateway, with Tailscale, multi-machine profiles, and Leash approvals.
• Hermes-Relay: cloud “agent in pocket.” Hermes Mobile pairs directly via QR, LAN, or Tailscale — no cloud credits.
• Hermex / HermesPilot: other native clients. Hermes Mobile uses gateway API + QR pair + Leash Pro + honest connection state.
