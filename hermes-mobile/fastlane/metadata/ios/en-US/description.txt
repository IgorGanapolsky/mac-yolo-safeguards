Hermes Mobile is the phone remote for a Hermes agent on YOUR Mac, Linux, or Windows. Chat with your gateway, see honest connection and run state, switch machines, and approve or deny risky tool calls before they execute.

ONE PRICE. ALL FEATURES.
• Pay once $9.99 on the App Store - no monthly Leash subscription
• Live chat with your Hermes gateway
• Leash: approve or deny blocked tools from your phone
• Standing allow/block gate rules
• ThumbGate feedback on replies and decisions
• QR pairing and saved computer profiles - no Hermes Mobile cloud account

BUILT FOR AI CODING AGENT OPERATORS
Control the Hermes gateway that sits beside Cursor, Claude Code, Codex, Gemini CLI, Copilot, Windsurf, OpenClaw, and similar tools. Hermes Mobile does not replace those products - it supervises the agent on your computer.

REAL CONNECTION STATE
• Home Wi-Fi locally; Tailscale or your tunnel remotely
• Clear reconnecting / stale / unavailable states
• API keys stay on the computer that runs the gateway

Camera access is only for pairing QR codes. Optional analytics can be disabled in Settings.

Requires a compatible Hermes gateway you operate. Independent - not affiliated with Nous Research, Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Replit, GitHub, Cursor, Windsurf, or OpenClaw.
