How it works

The background LaunchAgent daemon runs locally on your Mac. If your agent enters an infinite loop, leaks memory, or freezes simulator targets, the watchdog intercepts and reaps the processes while keeping your GUI safe.

❯ tail -f ~/Library/Logs/hermes-watchdog.log
[2026-07-05 18:32:00] WATCHDOG active (PID 19452)
[2026-07-05 18:32:45] WARNING: process 'claude' (PID 19804) CPU at 96% for 60s
[2026-07-05 18:33:45] REAPED process 'claude' (PID 19804) — Stuck loop detected
[2026-07-05 18:33:46] Notification dispatched: "Run reaped safely"
