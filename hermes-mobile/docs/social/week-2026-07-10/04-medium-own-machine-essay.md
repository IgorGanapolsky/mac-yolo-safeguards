# Medium — Own-machine essay

**Subtitle:** Why I built a phone remote for desktop AI agents instead of joining the cloud credit economy

Cloud agents centralize risk: your keys, your spend, your blast radius. Hermes Mobile is the opposite — compute on **your** hardware, **Leash** gate before destructive tools, phone UI when you're away from the desk.

Not Hermes Agent (AI on Android). Not Hermes-Relay (cloud middleman). **Your machine, your rules.**
