## The problem

Cloud coding agents meter your time. Phone AI apps can't approve a blocked shell command on your Mac. If you already run **Cursor, Claude Code, Codex, or OpenClaw** locally, you need a **control plane**, not another chatbot.

## What Hermes Mobile does

1. Install Hermes gateway on your Mac (free, open source)
2. Scan the QR code from Hermes Mobile on your phone
3. Chat with your agent; when a risky tool call is blocked, tap **Approve** or **Deny**

Your API keys never leave your hardware. Works over Wi‑Fi, USB, Tailscale, or tunnel.

## What it is NOT

- Not ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini on your phone
- Not a Replit-style cloud IDE
- Not running models on Android
