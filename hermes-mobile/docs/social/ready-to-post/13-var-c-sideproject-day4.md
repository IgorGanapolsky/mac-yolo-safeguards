## Title

Hermes Mobile — phone remote for AI agents on your own Mac (approve/deny Leash, honest free tier)

## Body

Built this after one too many "agent ran `rm -rf` while I was at dinner" moments.

**Hermes Mobile** = Android app that talks to a Hermes gateway on **your Mac**:

- Chat with your gateway from the phone (free)
- Leash tab: see blocked tool calls, approve or deny — **10 free approvals/week**, $19.99/mo Pro for unlimited
- Pair via QR / Tailscale — not a cloud IDE meter
