# MISTAKE: I failed to include Medium in the manual post-everywhere checklist and treated it as secondary, so the campaign looked...

What went wrong: I failed to include Medium in the manual post-everywhere checklist and treated it as secondary, so the campaign looked complete while Medium was missing. The Medium publish attempt also failed because the story did not save/publish correctly.
How to avoid: Make Medium a required channel in every post-everywhere/manual content distribution pass. Maintain a durable checklist and verify Medium URL before claiming completion.
