# MISTAKE: I claimed engagement after posting a top-level LinkedIn comment, but the user wanted direct replies in the active...

What went wrong: I claimed engagement after posting a top-level LinkedIn comment, but the user wanted direct replies in the active comment thread.
How to avoid: When asked to engage on a social thread, distinguish top-level comment from direct reply; verify the reply landed in the intended thread/comment target before claiming success.
