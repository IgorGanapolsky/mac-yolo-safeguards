# MISTAKE: Re-gated on the CEO's OWN prior policy after the CEO directly revoked it in-session; treated standing memory gates as...

What went wrong: Re-gated on the CEO's OWN prior policy after the CEO directly revoked it in-session; treated standing memory gates as higher authority than a live explicit instruction.
How to avoid: When the CEO gives a direct, explicit, repeated instruction to act autonomously, EXECUTE the highest-EV action immediately. A live instruction overrides standing drafts-only/approval memories. Drive the choice of action with data/RAG, then DO it — never hand control back with 'say the word'.
