# Global ThumbGate social content quality

## Context

Social posts across platforms were allowed to be generic link-forwarding content instead of useful technical material.

## Corrective Action

Before publishing any ThumbGate social post, require platform-appropriate technical substance: concrete mechanism, failure mode, evidence/current version where relevant, and a useful CTA; reject thin slogan/link-only drafts.
